Frequently asked questions
What is Mega Mind Co. (MMC)?
Mega Mind Co. (MMC) is an innovation hub focused on empowering creativity, fostering collaboration, and developing solutions that drive meaningful change. We work across a wide range of projects, integrating advanced technology and social impact to create sustainable and scalable solutions.
What types of projects does MMC work on?
MMC works on a variety of projects across three main branches:
1.) Core: Major revenue-generating projects, such as SMDX, Postlite, and BuildScrape.
2.) Nexus: Partial-profit projects that combine business revenue with social impact, including MOOCs, open research conferences, and hackathons.
3.) Foundation: Non-profit projects focused on social service, like workshops on cybersecurity, mental health, and educational initiatives like Pen & Kalam. How does MMC support social causes?
Through the MMC Foundation, we support a wide range of social initiatives. These include awareness campaigns, educational workshops, and creative programs aimed at addressing societal issues like mental health, cybersecurity, and literacy. The Foundation ensures that a significant portion of MMC’s work is dedicated to creating positive, long-lasting community impact.
How does MMC integrate research into its projects?
Research is embedded in every project at MMC. We take a multi-perspective approach, looking at the technical, social, economic, and environmental impacts of our work. Our R&D team conducts continuous research in areas like AI, sustainability, and social issues to back our projects with data, ensuring their viability, impact, and success.
How can I get involved with MMC?
You can get involved with MMC by joining our team and contributing to specific projects, or collaborating with us on research. We are always looking for passionate individuals and organizations to join us in driving innovation and creating solutions that matter. Visit our website or contact us for more details on how to become a part of the MMC community. You can also become a social volunteer at MMC Foundation

What is the structure of MMC?
MMC is organized into three main branches:
Core: Focused on driving revenue-generating projects.
Nexus: Serves as a bridge between the Core and Foundation, supporting projects with both social and commercial aspects.
Foundation : A non-profit division dedicated to social service and creating positive societal change through education, awareness, and community engagement.
How does MMC ensure the success of its projects?
We ensure the success of our projects by combining cutting-edge technology, innovative research, and a strong focus on collaboration. Each project is carefully evaluated from multiple perspectives, including its commercial viability, social impact, and long-term sustainability. Our focus on continuous learning and adaptation helps us achieve consistent, positive results.
